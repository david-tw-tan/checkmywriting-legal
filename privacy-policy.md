# Privacy Policy for Check My Writing

**Last updated: Oct 9, 2025**

## Information We Collect

Check My Writing collects and processes the following information:

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: When you create an account or sign in
- **Display Name**: Optional name you choose to display
- **Writing Samples**: Photos and text you upload for analysis

### Usage Information
- **App Usage**: How you interact with the app features
- **Analytics Data**: App performance and crash reports (anonymized)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for technical support)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Services**: Analyze your writing and provide feedback
- **Improve App**: Enhance features and fix technical issues
- **Communication**: Send important app updates and notifications
- **Account Management**: Maintain your subscription and preferences

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage**: Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud)
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel can access your data

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase**: For data storage and authentication
- **Apple App Store**: For subscription processing
- **OpenAI**: For AI-powered writing analysis

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Portability**: Export your data in a standard format

## Children's Privacy

Check My Writing is designed for educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
